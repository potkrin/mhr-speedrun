About submission

  • You can submit any records you know(including under top 3 records) if the record is valid.
  • The records needs a video either on Youtube, Niconico, or Bilibili.
  • Submission will appear immediately on the list!
  • This site doesn't check the validation of the video contents(link miss, breaking rules, etc...), so please be careful on the submission.
  • If you find problems about the record on the list, please submit issues from issue button.
To submit the record you know,
  1. Go to quest list page and click the quest of the record.
  2. Check the record list that your submission is still not on the list.
  3. Click the submit button on top of the list, and fill the form.
  4. Check the information you filled and submit!
